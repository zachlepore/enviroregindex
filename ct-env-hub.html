Recent Updates

Release-Based Cleanup Regs (RBCRs) — Effective

RBCRs took effect March 1, 2026, replacing the CT Property Transfer Act with a release-based reporting and cleanup system.

March 1, 2026

MS4 General Permit — Draft Reissuance

CT DEEP issued a Notice of Tentative Determination to reissue the MS4 General Permit; public comment period opened March 25, 2025.

March 25, 2025

REACT Portal Launch

CT DEEP's new Release, Environmental Assessment, and Cleanup Tracker (REACT) portal is live for document uploads and tracking as of early 2026.

CT Stormwater Quality Manual — Updated

Revised manual incorporates updated LID guidance, new water quality storm sizing (1.3"), and updated BMP performance criteria.
